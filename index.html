Rakshak Foundation - a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization and headquartered in Santa Clara, California - researches different public policy issues and creates awareness about them. The basic objective of Rakshak is to help strengthen the foundations of our society and bolster the confidence of a common man in the system by creating awareness about the issues affecting him. Read more

Rakshak Foundation is actively engaged and seeks volunteers in the following areas (click on the project for details):

  • Legal and Social Research
  • Accountability and Responsibility in Journalism
  • Promoting Responsible Citizenry
  • Improving Quality of Life - Infrastructure, Traffic, Pollution and Basic Necessities
  • Improving Accountability, Efficiency and Transparency of Public Offices
  • Nationalism, Social Responsibility and Civil Society through Education
  • Promoting positive aspects of our culture and society
